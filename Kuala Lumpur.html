Discover Kuala Lumpur: The Capital City Of Malaysia

Kuala Lumpur ，The city serves as the cultural， financial， tourism， political and economic centre of Malaysia.

Petronas Twin Tower

In the morning, stop by the famous Petronas Twin Towers, where you have the opportunity to visit the iconic towers of Malaysia. Don’t forget to arrive early to avoid the bustling crowds and head over to the observation deck to take in the stunning views of the city.

Suria KLCC

Suria'To soak in the evening sun, find a comfortable spot at the KLCC Park, where you can also opt to take a leisurely walk and take in the park’s beautiful surroundings. Alternatively, enjoy a picnic or relax by the Lake Symphony Water and the lush greenery the park has to offer. To top off the evening, stay for the Fountain Show, which takes place right in front of the Petronas Twin Towers.

Changkat Bukit Bintang

Come evenings, Changkat Bukit Bintang is the place to be. This popular nightlife spot is home to some of the best cocktails and drinks in the city. Dine at some of the coolest restaurants and unwind at one of the many vibrant bars in the area. Option also to end your night of exploring Bukit Bintang at Jalan Alor, known for its street dining and local delights.

Merdeka Square

“The Birthplace of Malaysia” At midnight on 31 August 1957 became Malysia independent nation. A flag was hoisted on the giant 100 metres (328 ft) flagpole on Merdeka Square – an 8.2 hectare area of great historical interest.

Kuala Lumpur Forest Eco Park

Head over to the KL Forest Eco Park to bask in the morning sun under some of the lushest, shadiest trees the park has to offer. After relaxing at KL Forest Eco Park, visit KL Tower to take in the bird’s eye view of beautiful Kuala Lumpur from the tower. In the afternoon, visit the KL Bird Park to discover a multitude of birds across different species.
